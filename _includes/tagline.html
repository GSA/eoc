Evidence Project Portal

The Evidence Project Portal allows Federal agencies to share opportunities for those outside the government to engage on evidence needs. It aims to develop, launch, and enhance cross-sector relationships to advance evidence-building activities.

Program Evaluation Services Subgroup

The Evaluation Officer Council partnered with the General Services Administration (GSA) to create a subgroup under MAS SIN 541611. Federal agencies can select from this group of qualified, pre-vetted contractors to design and carry out program evaluation activities to meet agency evidence-building needs.
